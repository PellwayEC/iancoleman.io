Donations

I don't accept donations

Since my projects are often the efforts of many people, most of which don't appear in the obvious places like code or issues, donating to projects I work on poses significant operational difficulties.

As a result, if you would like to support the work on this site financially you are encouraged to donate to one of the many groups that makes the internet a place amenable to sites such as this one.
